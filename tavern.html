Our Food

At Street Tavern, we take pride in showcasing the diverse and delicious cuisines of Africa. Our menu features carefully crafted dishes inspired by traditions from across the country using authentic recipes and the finest ingredients sourced locally and beyond.

Jollof Rice Special

Our signature Jollof Rice, cooked to perfection with aromatic spices and served with your choice of protein.

Traditional Egusi Soup

Rich and flavorful melon seed soup prepared with assorted meats and vegetables, served with pounded yam.

Grilled Croaker Fish

Fresh croaker fish, seasoned with our special spice blend and grilled to perfection.

Spicy Asun

Succulent chunks of grilled goat meat, tossed in a spicy pepper sauce with onions and bell peppers.

About Us

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience

Street Tavern is more than just a place to eat; it's a gathering place, a celebration of culture, and a taste of home. Our warm and inviting atmosphere, inspired by the vibrant spirit of Nigeria, provides the perfect backdrop for sharing delicious food and creating lasting memories with family and friends.
